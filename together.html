My Coding Journey & Aspirations

Hello! I'm Aadi Saini, a 7th-grade student from Rocklin, California, with a deep-seated passion for coding and technology. My journey into the world of programming started with a curiosity to understand how websites and applications are built, and it quickly grew into a dedicated pursuit.

I thrive on challenges, especially in competitive programming. This year, I was thrilled to clear both the USACO Bronze and Silver divisions, which has only fueled my desire to delve deeper into complex algorithms and data structures in C++. My goal is to tackle the Gold and Platinum divisions next!

Beyond C++, I'm actively expanding my skills in web development, working with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and exploring frameworks like React. I enjoy the creative process of bringing ideas to life on the web, from simple static pages to more interactive experiences.

The constant evolution of technology excites me. I believe in lifelong learning and am always eager to pick up new skills, experiment with different tools, and collaborate on interesting projects. The ability to solve problems and create something tangible through code is incredibly rewarding.
